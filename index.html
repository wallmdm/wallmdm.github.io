Loveless Cafe and Bar is an intimate cafe that offers a variety of speciality coffees, baked goods and alchoholic drinks. We are located in the heart of Little Portugal on Dundas, and have been serving the community for several years. During the day we operate as a cafe where you can enjoy a coffee and relax with friends, or get some work done. After 5pm, we ask that all patrons not use their laptops on the tables as we transition into a bar. Visit Loveless Cafe and Bar today!
